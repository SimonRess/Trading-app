## Game Inspiration

**Hanse – Die Expedition** (1994, Ascon) — a German-language strategic trading simulation set in the 13th-century Hanseatic League. The player starts as a merchant in Lübeck, builds a trade network across northern Europe (Riga, Malmö, Nowgorod), manages a fleet of ships, starts a family with generational inheritance, and occasionally fights pirates. Target: become mayor of Lübeck.

Key aesthetic traits: SVGA isometric city views, hand-painted NPC portraits, parchment/wood-panel UI, AdLib MIDI music.
